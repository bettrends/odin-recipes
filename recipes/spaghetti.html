Spaghetti from a Box

Description

Spaghetti from a box and sauce from a jar. Simple as that!

Ingredients

  • Water - enough to fully cover noodles
  • Spaghetti - as much as you want
  • Jar of spaghetti sauce, your favorite type

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
  2. Add noodles to the water. Stir so noodles do not get stuck.
  3. When noodles are tender, drain the water and return to pot. Add desired amount of sauce and enjoy.
