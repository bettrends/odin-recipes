Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

This is a buffalo chicken dip recipe I found online. It is a cheesy and not too spicy dip.

Ingredients

  • 10 oz chicken
  • 3/4 cup Franks Red Hot Sauce
  • 2 packages of cream cheese
  • 1 cup ranch dressing
  • 1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Shred/chop chicken into small pieces. If chicken is uncooked, cook before cutting.
  2. Add all ingredients to crock pot and turn on high.
  3. After 1 hour, stir all ingredients together and turn crock pot to low.
  4. Let cook until no big chunks remaining. Stir occasionally.
