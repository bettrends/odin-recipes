Rice Krispies Treats

Description

A basic recipe for rice krispies treats coming from Kellogs.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 package marshmallows
  • 6 cups rice krispies cereal

Steps

  1. In a pan, melt the butter. Once butter is melted, melt the marshmallows and stir until smooth.
  2. Remove from heat and add rice krispies
  3. Spread evenly in a greased pan and allow to cool.
